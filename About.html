My story

Hello everyone, my name is Dai, a second-year Computer Engineering student at Da Nang University of Science and Technology.​ I am learning and developing my skills in programming, embedded systems, and artificial intelligence. I am passionate about technology and eager to explore AI and IoT applications in real-world scenarios.

Unlike many others who have a strong passion for technology from an early age, my decision to pursue this field was quite spontaneous. However, after a year and a half of studying, I have gradually developed a genuine interest in Computer Engineering and begun to appreciate its value.

After one and a half years, my perspective has changed significantly. At first, I felt uncertain and lacked enthusiasm, but now, I genuinely enjoy what I’m learning. My interest grew when I started taking core subjects such as C/C++ Programming. Some topics seemed dry at first, but as I delved deeper, I discovered how fascinating they were. One of my most memorable experiences was successfully programming an embedded device and seeing it function exactly as intended—it was a moment that made me realize the power of what I was learning.
